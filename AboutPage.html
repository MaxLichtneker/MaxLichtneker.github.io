ABOUT

Hiya! I'm Gabby, a 2D animator and illustrator based in the Netherlands. I'm an artist with an all-round love for frame-by-frame animation, comics, character design, and ghosts :]

My work is mainly character driven, and I like exploring little parts of myself through these characters by putting them through strange situations.

I'm familiar with TvPaint, Clip Studio Paint, Storyboard Pro, Photoshop, Toonboom Harmony, Premier, and a little bit of After Effects.

I have experience as a 2D rough animator, inbetweener and clean-up artist, and I'm always eager to learn new skills!

Please feel free to contact me via my email at gabriella019@hotmail.com, or any of these socials below!
