I’m Gabby, a 25 year old 2D animator based in the Netherlands, and a graduate from the Masters of Animation at AKV St Joost.

I’m an artist with an all-round love for animation, comics, character design and wizards. My work mainly focuses on characters and the stories I can tell with them, and I try to put Lgbtqa+ themes in my work whenever I can.

I’m currently looking for work as a 2D character animator or clean-up artist, but I’m open to almost everything and incredibly eager to learn new skills!

I’m most familiar with TvPaint, Storyboard Pro, Premier and Photoshop, but I also have experience in Toonboom Harmony and a little bit of After Effects.
