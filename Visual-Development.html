Dearly Departed Fly Sincerely Snip Memento Mori Douse

Alfred Jodocus Kwak- character design - 2023

This work was made as part of a collaboration between Submarine and St. Joost for the program of Project Soho. Project Soho is a 10-week program that offers St. Joost Master of Animation students hands-on experience with working in the context of a studio setting and dealing with real clients. As part of the Soho-Submarine collaboration, we worked on character designs and environment explorations for a hypothetical remake of Alfred Jodocus Kwak.
